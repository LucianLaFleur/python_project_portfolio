Zhiqiao  (Chinese: 芷巧 Zhiqiǎo) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the World Quest A Teapot to Call Home: Part I.
In the quest, she signed a contract with Krosl to rent some Smaragdus Jadeite.
