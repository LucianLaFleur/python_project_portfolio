Profile[]
Ivano is a Fatui soldier in The Chasm. He and a fellow soldier, a Fatui Cryo Cicin Mage, were sent to strike a trade deal for food with a group of Treasure Hoarders in the Lumberpick Valley, led by Brass Bull Zhao and Chen the Flower. However, the deal was interrupted by the Traveler, who was assisting the Millelith in stopping the Treasure Hoarders' illegal activities, and Ivano and his comrade were defeated alongside the Treasure Hoarders.

Appearance[]
Ivano takes the form of a Fatui Pyro Agent.
