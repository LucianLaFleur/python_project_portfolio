Aru (Japanese: 亜琉 Aru), also known as Argos, is an open-world NPC located in Enkanomiya, Inazuma.

Profile[]
Aru is a sinless Sinshade specifically created to facilitate rituals. He is specifically in charge of explaining how to restore the Reins of Revival[1] needed to access the depths of Enkanomiya where the Jade Coral Branch can be found.

Etymology[]
