Mo (Chinese: 老墨 Lǎo Mò) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Hu Tao's Story Quest Papilio Charontis Chapter: Act I - Yet the Butterfly Flutters Away. He can be found inside the Story Domain Enter the "border" with Shu'yang.
