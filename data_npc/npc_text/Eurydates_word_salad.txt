Profile[]
According to Ema, Eurydates was from a wealthy and distinguished clan of Byakuyakoku.[1] According to Date, his father used crutches and used them to beat Date up because his writing "lacked rhyme or rhetoric."[2]

Trivia[]
Eurybates is a name of several Trojan War characters, and was adapted by John Racine as "Eurydates" for his tragedies.
