Otto is an open-world NPC located in the City of Mondstadt, Mondstadt. He can be found guarding the entrance to the Favonius Cathedral.
During the Archon Quest "Venti's New Plan," he will instead stand guard at the entrance to the Cathedral's basement at daytime.

Appearance[]
Otto wears one of the standard uniforms of the Knights of Favonius. The uniform consists of a black long-sleeve shirt and horse riding pants with white plate armor worn on top. However, the poleyns, greaves, and sabatons are black. The Knights of Favonius crest decorates the breastplate.
