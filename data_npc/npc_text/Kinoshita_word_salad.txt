Kinoshita is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears alongside Okawara Goemon in Chapter II, Act I: The Immovable God and the Eternal Euthymia.
He appeared in the story domain Police Detention Center. Together with Okawara Goemon, he guarded Thoma during the Vision Hunt Ceremony.
