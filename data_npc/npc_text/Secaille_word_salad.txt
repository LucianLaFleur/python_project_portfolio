Profile[]
Secaille is a Meropide guard. She helps the Traveler uncover the scheme involving the Beret Society. She also comforts Eclancher to help her recover after her traumatizing experience with the Aqua Doloris. Secaille is met in the domain Dougier's "Kingdom" along with Eclancher and helps evacuate and rescue the people there.[1]

Appearance[]
Secaille has blue hair and green eyes and wears the black uniform of the Meropide guards.
