Araminali is an Aranara and NPC located in Vissudha Field, south-east of Vanarana (Subarea), Sumeru. Located underground and rewards you with 8 Common Chests, 3 Exquisite Chests, 3 Precious Chests, and 1 Luxurious Chest as a reward for helping all 64 unnamed Aranara of Dharma Forest. Opening all chests rewards you with the For Meritorious Service achievement.
