Dadao is a Redbill Pelican that can be found in Chenyu Vale: Upper Vale, Chenyu Vale, Liyue.
Interacting with Dadao for the first time gives the player Fish Seized From a Pelican's Mouth. It can be used on the food dish next to Daju, which rewards an Exquisite Chest.
