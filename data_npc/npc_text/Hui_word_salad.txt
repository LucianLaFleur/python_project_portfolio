Hui is an open-world NPC in Liyue, near the Statue of The Seven at Dihua Marsh, Bishui Plain. He can be found on top of the rock near Hulu's stall.

Profile[]
Hui is a backstabber who claims to be from out of town.

Appearance[]
Hui is an adult man of average height and build with black hair and blue eyes. His hair is short and slicked to the right side of his face. He wears an outfit with an assortment of gray, black, brown, and white accents.
