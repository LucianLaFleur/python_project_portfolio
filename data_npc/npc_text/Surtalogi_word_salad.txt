Surtalogi, also known as The Foul, is a mentioned character in Genshin Impact. He is Skirk's master, the owner of the All-Devouring Narwhal and one of the Five Sinners of Khaenri'ah.

Profile[]
Surtalogi is first mentioned by Skirk in the Archon Quest The Opera of Noirceur and Blancheur in Chapter IV: Act V - Masquerade of the Guilty.
Surtalogi is the master of Skirk and the owner of the All-Devouring Narwhal, who he sees as a pet. When asked about what kind of entity Surtalogi is, Skirk states that he is someone similar to Rhinedottir and Vedrfolnir in "pursuing some form of perfection."[1]

Trivia[]
Etymology[]
Surtalogi is the name of the bright sword or flame wielded by the Fire Jotunn Surtr from Norse Mythology.
