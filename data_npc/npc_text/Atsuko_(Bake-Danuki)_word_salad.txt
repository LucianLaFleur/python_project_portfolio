Atsuko is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the World Quest Home Lies Over the Ocean. While taking on the name of Atsuko, they are actually a Bake-Danuki.
The Bake-Danuki developed feelings for Atsuko's parents after they took care of its wounds. Knowing how much they worry for their daughter who is traveling abroad, it disguised itself as her to live together with them. It left after almost being exposed by the Traveler.
