Trivia[]
Etymology[]
"Arayasa" is derived from Vyasa, traditionally regarded as the author of the Sanskrit epic Mahabharata.
Other Aranaras involved in Encounter in the Woods are also named after poets in ancient India, fitting their roles as song-gatherers.
