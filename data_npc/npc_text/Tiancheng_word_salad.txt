  Event-Exclusive NPCs 




Tiancheng (Chinese: 天成 Tiānchéng) is a member of the Millelith. He is an event-exclusive NPC who makes an appearance in the Event Story Quest The Crisis Deepens in Unreconciled Stars: Act II. During this event, he can be found at Wangshu Inn.
