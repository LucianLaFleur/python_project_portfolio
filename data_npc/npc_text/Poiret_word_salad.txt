Profile[]
Poiret is an inmate in the Fortress of Meropide. In the fortress, he teams with Genevieve to play Coupon Millionaire. During Game of the Rich, Poiret's team gives their points to Rave. Poiret and Rave ultimately lose to the Kids team (Traveler, Caterpillar).[1] In Both Brains and Brawn, Poiret and Genevieve team up again to compete in the Pankration tournament and they are coached by the Traveler.[2]
