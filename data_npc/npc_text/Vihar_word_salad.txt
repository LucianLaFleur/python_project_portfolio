Profile[]
Vihar was tasked of roleplaying as the Knight of Flowers during the Sabzeruz Festival. He accepted the position because he wanted to see Dunyarzad, whom he likes, happy. During the festival he invited Paimon and the Traveler in a guessing game to earn candies.
