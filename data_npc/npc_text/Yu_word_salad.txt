Yu is an open-world NPC who can be found in Dihua Marsh, Liyue. He is friends with Mengmeng but, because of his father's illness and his subsequent taking over of their shop, no longer has the time to play with her. Yu carries a bag with him all the time. The content of it is unknown.

Appearance[]
Yu has black, short hair with bangs, dark blue eyes and black glasses. He is a child, that's why he has a small body. Yu wears a blue coat with short sleeves and a light grayish blue blouse with a high collar and black buttons. On the legs, he's wearing black, knee length pants, white socks a little bellow the knees and black with dark blue shoes.
