Kaedehara Hisamichi (Japanese: 楓原久通 Kaedehara Hisamichi) was Kaedehara Kazuha's grandfather who died before the start of the game. Kazuha's memories with him appear during the Event Story Quest As the Courtyard in Spring Once Appeared in Summertime Odyssey:  II - As the Courtyard in Spring Once Appeared.
