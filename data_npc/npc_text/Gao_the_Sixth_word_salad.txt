Gao the Sixth (Chinese: 高老六 Gāo Lǎo Liù) is an event-exclusive NPC who makes an appearance in the Event Story Quest What the Skies Conceal, the Water Reveals in Unreconciled Stars: Act III. During this event, he can be found in Qingce Village.
