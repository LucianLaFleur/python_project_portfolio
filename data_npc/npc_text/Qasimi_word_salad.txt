Profile[]
He is one of Dori's business partners, whom Dori introduces Kaveh and the Traveler to in order to secure funding for the construction of Badawi's library.

Etymology[]
Qasimi may be a reference to Kasim (Arabic: قاسم Qāsim) from One Thousand and One Nights, the rich and greedy merchant brother of the poor Ali Baba.
