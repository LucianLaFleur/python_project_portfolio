Profile[]
Domenico is a prisoner in the Fortress of Meropide who sent a blackmail letter to Kiara, threatening to kidnap her. Domenico also once tried to rouse the prisoners' resentment and hatred to incite a color revolution against Wriothesley and Neuvillette. Eventually, Domenico confessed to his crimes and sending the letter, and Wriothesley has Sigewinne take Domenico away to the detention center.[1]
