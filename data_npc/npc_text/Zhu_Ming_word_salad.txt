Zhu Ming (Chinese: 祝明 Zhù Míng) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Hu Tao's Story Quest Papilio Charontis Chapter: Act I - Yet the Butterfly Flutters Away. He can be found inside the Story Domain Enter the "border." As a newcomer, he is impressed with how astonishing the place looks.
