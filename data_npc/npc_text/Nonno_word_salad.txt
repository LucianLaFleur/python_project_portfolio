Nonno (Ainu: ノンノ) is a quest-exclusive NPC who appears at Tsurumi Island after the player completes the World Quest The Sun-Wheel and Mt. Kanna.

Profile[]
She is a long lost soul who likes to play hide and seek.

Appearance[]
Nonno is, like any other interactable lost soul on Tsurumi island, a light blue, almost transparent figure with a white, glowing point at the place of her heart. She doesn't have any limbs or any details on her face.

Trivia[]
"Nonno" (Ainu: ノンノ non-no) is the Ainu word for "flower."
