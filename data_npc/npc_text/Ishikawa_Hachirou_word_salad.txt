Ishikawa Hachirou (Japanese: 石川八郎 Ishikawa Hachirou) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the Inazuma Commission Bantan Sango Case File: The Right Identity and Inazuma Commission Bantan Sango Case File: Case-Closing Time. He seems to have lost his Vision, though it is implied during the former Commission that he is faking it.
