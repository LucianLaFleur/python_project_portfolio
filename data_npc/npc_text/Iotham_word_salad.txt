Iotham is an open-world NPC located in Vimara Village, Sumeru who first appears during the Aranyaka World Quest The Children of Vimara Village.
His dialogue changes based on the player's progress through Aranyaka. Following a certain dialogue branch for the first time after completing Aranyaka gives the player four Starshrooms.
