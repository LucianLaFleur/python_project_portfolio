Caspar is an open-world NPC in Liyue who originally came from Fontaine. He can be found near the Wangshu Inn, admiring the scenery.

Trivia[]
Caspar is a reference to German Romanticist painter Caspar David Friedrich who was known for his landscape paintings, his most famous painting being "Wanderer above the Sea of Fog." During a Lantern Rite Event Quest he sits together with Holderlin, who is in turn a reference to German Romanticist philosopher Friedrich Hölderlin.
