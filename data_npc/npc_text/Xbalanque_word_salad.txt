Trivia[]
Xbalanque is the name of one of the Maya Hero Twins. Due to the name's origin in Mesoamerican mythology, it is reasonable to presume that the game character Xbalanque is from Natlan.
Xbalanque is pronounced  /ʃɓalaŋˈke/ "sh-bah-lahn-KAY."
