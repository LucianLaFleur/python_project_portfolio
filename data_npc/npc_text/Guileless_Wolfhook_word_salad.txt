Profile[]
Wolfhook is one of the five Fruity Order Operatives. Wolfhook views the order as a family. Moreover, Wolfhook believes there is no need to vote as family members are supposed to look after another, regardless of who is the majority or minority.

Appearance[]
He has purple hair, blue eyes, and a purple coat.
