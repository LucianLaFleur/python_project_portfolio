Profile[]
Pharez is a Sumeru Akademiya student from the Kshahrewar Darshan. Despite belonging to separate Darshans, they collaborate with Amurta student Yasmin and Spantamad student Ladep to study the health of Sumeru City's Divine Tree.[1]
