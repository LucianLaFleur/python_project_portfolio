Profile[]
Dalong has a younger sister named Little Le and wanted to get one of Chongyun's popsicles for her to try. Chongyun says his popsicles are rather tasteless, but Dalong still persists. Dalong's mother is Yuyan. When playing hide and seek, he is located behind a crate at the back alleyway of the left side of Chen the Sharp's food stand.

Appearance[]
Dalong is a small boy with light brown hair and grayish eyes, uses a traditional Chinese style shirt, shorts and high boots.
