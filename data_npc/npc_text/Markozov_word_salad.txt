Profile[]
Markozov is the leader of a group of Fatui stationed in the Girdle of the Sands, claiming to seek "knowledge" for his own pursuits. He attempts to claim Sorush, stating that she has the knowledge he desires, but is driven off by the Traveler. Eventually, they would run into each other again while the Traveler was investigating Fatui camps. Eventually, he is defeated a second time and withdraws his entire forces from the region for the time being.

Appearance[]
Markozov takes on the appearance of a Fatui Skirmisher - Electrohammer Vanguard.
