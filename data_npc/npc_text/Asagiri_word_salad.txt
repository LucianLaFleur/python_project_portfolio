Profile[]
Asagiri is a freelance illustrator who draws light novel illustrations for the Yae Publishing House, including illustrations for Fukumoto's light novel King of Invokations. Miss Moriko is the executive editor in charge of her work.[1] Besides light novels, she has also illustrated various other things, such as a character for a scientific publication[2] and various promotional materials for Xavier's film "The Seventh Samurai."[3][4]

Appearance[]
Asagiri has long black hair and fair skin, and wears a light blue kimono patterned with pink sakura flowers and a dark blue obi.
