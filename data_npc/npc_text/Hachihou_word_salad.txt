"Hachihou"  (Japanese: 八宝) is a Northland Cat and NPC located at the Asase Shrine, Seirai Island, Inazuma.

Profile[]
Neko describes "Hachihou" as a very agile little fellow, who very fond of fish. For this reason, it will sometimes go to the seaside to look for fish that have washed up on the shore.[1]

Trivia[]
"Hachihou" is likely named after the concept of Ashtamangala, a suite of eight auspicious symbols associated with enlightenment; originating from Hinduism and other Dharmic religions.
