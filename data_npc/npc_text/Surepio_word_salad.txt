Etymology[]
Surepio's name is likely derived from Asclepius, the Greek god of medicine; the Rod of Asclepius continues to symbolize medicine and health care even in modern times. Asclepius was one of the sons of the sun god Apollo, fitting with the naming scheme of the other Sunchildren.
