Profile[]
Tariq scams the Traveler into giving him 500,000 Mora. However, Alhaitham reveals that Tariq is a fraud and gets him to return that Mora. Tariq then challenges Alhaitham to a duel. But at the duel, the Traveler and Alhaitham defeat Tariq and his acquaintances.[1]
