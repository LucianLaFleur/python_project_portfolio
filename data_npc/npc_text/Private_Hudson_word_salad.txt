Profile[]
It's done, man! It's done! What should we do now?—Private Hudson, after his defeat
While raiding a Fatui hideout in the Desert of Hadramaveth, where Fatui classified documents stated Jeht had been taken to for her transfer back to Snezhnaya, the Traveler encounters and defeats Private Hudson. It is unclear whether they kill him or not.

Appearance[]
Private Hudson takes on the appearance of a Cryogunner Legionnaire

Etymology[]
Like a few other characters featured in the Her Foes Rage Like Great Waters... Quest Series, Private Hudson is a shout-out to a movie character. He is a reference to Private Hudson from the movie Aliens, and more specifically to his famous quote "Game over, man! Game over! What [...] are we gonna do now?" (expletives omitted).
