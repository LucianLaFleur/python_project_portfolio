Xiuhua is an open-world NPC in Liyue Harbor. She can found near the docks, standing by the entrance of the Baiju Guesthouse.
Her twin sister is Yuhua, a waitress at the Wangshu Inn. From time to time, the two will switch places at their respective establishments.
