Dialogue[]

Vaneigem: Fine art and good food. Ah, what a wonderful life...
 Hello there.
Vaneigem: It is a pleasure to have met you on this fine day, dear guest. My name is Vaneigem, and I am the owner of this establishment.
Vaneigem: If you have any comments or suggestions regarding the menu or performances, please do speak your mind. I will do my best to provide you with more comprehensive services.
 Tell me about art...
Vaneigem: As you know, we often invite artists to perform here at our restaurant. The most popular of these are the magical performers Mr. Lyney and Ms. Lynette.
Vaneigem: "Art is the key ingredient in fine dining" — this is something that I believe in most strongly, and I hope that the cultural atmosphere here shall spread far and wide.
 Impressive.
Vaneigem: Thank you so much for your appreciation, dear guest. If you don't mind, please accept this sweet treat as a personal thank-you gift.

Trivia[]
Vaneigem is named after Raoul Vaneigem, a Belgian writer known writing one of the two most influential texts of the Situationist movement, the other being written by Guy Debord, for whom Hotel Debord is named.
