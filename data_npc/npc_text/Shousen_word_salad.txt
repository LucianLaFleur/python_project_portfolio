Trivia[]
Iwakura Mitsunari mentions him if you talk to him after defeating the other Iwakura Art disciples, wondering if Shousen taught the Traveler their swordsmanship style.
During the Hyakunin Ikki event of Version 2.5, Kayabuki Ikkei mentions that even Shousen was impressed by the Traveler's performance and persistence in the last tournament.
Kid Kujirai mentions him, saying that Kid Kujirai and Alice created "Kujirai Art, Temari Jutsu" with his advice.
