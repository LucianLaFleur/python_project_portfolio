Profile[]
Wyler is a member of the Beret Society. He gets captured by the Traveler and Wriothesley. Wriothesley then forces Wyler and Gouzien to give out the real location of the Beret Society's headquarters, saying that he will consider this when handing out Wyler's sentence and punishment.
