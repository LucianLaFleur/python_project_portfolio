Profile[]
Bouhanu is an Eremite Mercenary sent to assassinate the Traveler under the idea that Jeht sent them to "keep the Traveler company".

Appearance[]
Bouhanu take on the appearance of an Eremite Sword-Dancer with darker skin, brown hair and differently designed shirt and pants.
