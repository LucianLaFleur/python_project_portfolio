Liang (Chinese: 良子 Liángzi) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the Archon Quest Chapter II: Prologue - Autumn Winds, Scarlet Leaves. 
He is a disciple of the founder of the Lingshan Clan.

Profile[]
He and his fellow disciple, Chai Yi, participated in The Crux Clash in the hopes of winning the Masterless Vision. He lost due to forfeit when he missed his scheduled match time while helping Chai Yi, who was knocked out cold by his opponent.
