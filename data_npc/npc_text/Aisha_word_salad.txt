Profile[]
Aisha was a talented professor and researcher with the Amurta Darshan of the Sumeru Akademiya. Twenty years before present, Professor Aisha gave Kathya a plant. Aisha was Kathya's tutor and a friend of her parents. Kathya describes Professor Aisha as an outstanding Amurta researcher. She was skilled in academics and combat. She worked a lot for the Adventurers' Guild.
When Kathya was 10 years old, Aisha left to explore the secrets of the Abyss. Aisha left a seed to Kathya, saying that when it blooms, she would return. However, Nahida and the Traveler determined that the plant would never bloom. Nahida says that the plant was a new species Aisha created that was unknown to the Akademia.[1]

Trivia[]
Aisha (Arabic: عائشة) was the name of the third wife of the Islamic prophet Muhammad. She is portrayed as intelligent, curious, and scholarly.
