Chai Yi (Chinese: 柴毅 Chái Yì) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the Archon Quest A Path Through the Storm in Chapter II: Prologue - Autumn Winds, Scarlet Leaves.

Profile[]
Chai Yi is a disciple of the founder of the Lingshan Clan. He and his fellow disciple, Liang, participated in The Crux Clash in the hopes of winning the Masterless Vision. He lost when he tried to deflect a punch with the Liangshan Clan's technique "force dispersal,"  but was instead punched in the nose.
