This article is about the eremite sentry in the entrance to Bayt Al-Sukr. For the eremite sentry in the entrance to Bayt Al-Muazzam, see Eremite Sentry (2).
Eremite Sentry is an open-world NPC located in Desert of Hadramaveth, Sumeru encountered after completing World Quest Dune-Entombed Fecundity: Part III in Series The Dirge of Bilqis. He is part of the Tanit tribe.
He guards the entrance to Bayt Al-Sukr, an entrance leading to the Eternal Oasis.
After completing For Her Judgment Reaches to the Skies...‍[verification needed] the sentry will disappear from his post.

The Eremite Sentry was ordered by Matriarch Babel to guard the entrances to two places leading to the Eternal Oasis.

Appearance[]
The Eremite Sentry takes on the appearance of an Eremite Crossbow enemy.

Thoughts[]
Eremite Sentry's thoughts can be accessed by using Nahida's Elemental Skill, All Schemes to Know.
