Profile[]
Jurieu is a researcher who worked as Edwin Eastinghouse's assistant, and Lourvine worked as one of Jurieu's assistants. Like Edwin, Jurieu researched Arkhium and gravimeters. Jurieu was then hired to become Wriothesley's technical consultant.[2]
