Daipai (Chinese: 戴派 Dàipài) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Across the Sea in Tartaglia's Story Quest, Monoceros Caeli Chapter: Act I - Mighty Cyclops' Adventure!.
During the first part, Across the Sea in Tartaglia's Story Quest, Monoceros Caeli Chapter: Act I - Mighty Cyclops' Adventure!, he was sent to patrol the area around Minlin while the Millelith investigate an active unattended Ruin Guard.
