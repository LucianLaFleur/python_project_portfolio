Profile[]
Jawad is an eremite mercenary who is working in Corps of Thirty. He was previously a Dakan Al-Ahmar member responsible for supplying their weapons and liaising their caravan. According to Shahzaman, he loves to hanging out in a tavern at night, for some drinks and a night breeze.

Appearance[]
Jawad takes an appearance with the Corps of Thirty outfit.
