Location[]
After completing the Event Story Quest Zip Along (According to Safe Cart Operating Procedures) in Secret Summer Paradise: Part I - An Invitation From Afar!, he can be found at the western stop of Violet track.

Jeroney's locationAdditional context
Profile[]
Like other characters in the Mirage (besides Idyia), the blue-haired Jeroney is a Hydro Eidolon replica of a human from Teyvat that once visited Mirage and then left it.[1]

Appearance[]
Like his brothers, Jeroney has short watery-blue hair, glowing blue eyes, and wears an Adventurers' Guild uniform. He also wears a black glasses.
