Susan is a quest-exclusive NPC located in Inazuma and Sumeru. She appears as part of the Click! and Clickety Click! World Quests, as such, she may appear in different areas.

Profile[]
Susan is a Mondstadt citizen who came to Inazuma with her husband due to his business trip. She is unable to leave due to the Sakoku Decree and is traveling to make the most of her stay. She is traveling alone as her husband is too lazy to join her. Once the Sakoku Decree lifted, Susan can be seen in Sumeru.

Appearance[]
Susan has shoulder-length black hair. She wears a red and white dress. Her accessories consist of a red choker necklace, and a red bracelet on both of her hands.
