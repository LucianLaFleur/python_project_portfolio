Huaqing  (Chinese: 华清 Huáqīng) is a member of the Millelith. He appears in the Archon Quest The Floating Palace in Chapter I: Act III - A New Star Approaches, where he is on guard duty at the Guizhong Ballista. During the event Unreconciled Stars, he could be found at Qingce Village.
