Niousha is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Yoimiya's Story Quest Carassius Auratus Chapter: Act II - Star-Pickers' Passage. She is the mother of Avin.

Profile[]
Traveler meets Niousha when Avin goes missing. The woman introduces herself as the girl's mother and asks the Traveler to find Avin. After Avin's Yoimiya's adventure in Mawtiyima Forest, the girl is returned home safely, and some time later gets a new treatment plan from Amurta doctors. When Yoimiya's Sumeru trip comes to an end, Niousha and Avin go to Port Ormos to bid their goodbyes.
