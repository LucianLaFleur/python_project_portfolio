  Quest-Exclusive NPCs 




Mysterious Shadow is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Sacrificial Offering. During the quest, she can be found at the abandoned shrine northwest of Konda Village, where the player must talk to her three clones until she teleports to the three Sky Kitsune Statues on the platform to proceed with the quest.
