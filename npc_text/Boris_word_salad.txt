Boris (Russian: Борис Boris) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Through the Motions, to the Heart in Eula's Story Quest, Aphros Delos Chapter: Act I - The Spindrift Shall Never Return to the Sea.
He can be found in the story domain Behind Enemy Lines.
