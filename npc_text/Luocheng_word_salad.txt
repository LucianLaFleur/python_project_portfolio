Luocheng (Chinese: 洛成 Luòchéng) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears during Hu Tao's Story Quest Papilio Charontis Chapter: Act I - Yet the Butterfly Flutters Away.
He comes seeking Hu Tao's help, for what he believes is a curse tormenting him.
