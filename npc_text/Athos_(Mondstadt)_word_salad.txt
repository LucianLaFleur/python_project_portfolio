Athos is a member of the Knights of Favonius located in the City of Mondstadt, Mondstadt. He guards the entrance of the Knights of Favonius Headquarters alongside Porthos.

Appearance[]
Athos wears one of the standard uniforms of the Knights of Favonius. The uniform consists of a black long-sleeve shirt and horse riding pants with white plate armor worn on top. However, the poleyns, greaves, and sabatons are black. The Knights of Favonius crest decorates the breastplate.

Trivia[]
Athos shares the same English voice actor with Lyney.
Etymology[]
Athos is named after one of the The Three Musketeers. The other characters who follow this naming convention are Porthos and Aramis.
