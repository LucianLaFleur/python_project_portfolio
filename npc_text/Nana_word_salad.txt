Profile[]
Nana is a Lady Poodle who currently serves as one of Penny's guardians, alongside Don Quijano. She was originally from the Court of Fontaine, where she was owned by Benoit Leroy and Autumn before Autumn took Nana alongside her to her second marriage.[1] At some point, Autumn left Nana with Penny.[2]

Trivia[]
Benoit's cat, also named Nana, inherited its name from the dog.[1]
Etymology[]
Nana is the name of the dog from the story, Peter Pan, who acts as a caretaker for the Darling children. The World Quest Free Verse like Peter Pan revolves around the theme of children growing up and completing the quest rewards the Wonders of the World achievement I Do Believe in Fairies, another Peter Pan reference.
