Ilyin (Russian: Ильин Il'in) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the World Quest Where the Dandelions Find Rest in Series The Very Special Fortune Slip. Although he has the appearance of a Fatui Skirmisher - Pyroslinger Bracer, he turns into the appearance of a Fatui Pyro Agent when fighting.
