Caishan (Chinese: 采珊 Cǎi Shān) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Knight and Deaconess, Ready for Battle! in Barbara's Hangout Event: Act I - Wellspring of Healing.

Profile[]
Caishan is a merchant from Liyue. She was traveling to Mondstadt when she was robbed and taken hostage by Treasure Hoarders north of the Whispering Woods. However, Barbara noticed the situation and managed to save her with the Traveler's help. In gratitude, Caishan offered some of her remaining items as a reward: Sweet Flowers, Liyue Chilibrew, or Mora.
