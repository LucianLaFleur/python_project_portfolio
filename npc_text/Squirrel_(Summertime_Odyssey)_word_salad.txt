Trivia[]
The title of the squirrel, "Strange Life-Form Here" (Chinese: 神奇生物在这里 lit. "Here Are the Fantastic Life-Forms"), is a reference to J. K. Rowling's famous book Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them, which is translated into Chinese as 神奇动物在哪里 lit. "Where Are the Fantastic Animals".
