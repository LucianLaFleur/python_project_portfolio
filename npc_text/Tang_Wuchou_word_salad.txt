Tang Wuchou (Chinese: 唐无仇 Táng Wúchóu) is a Treasure Hoarder from Liyue. He is located in Cinnabar Cliff, The Chasm.

Profile[]
Tang Wuchou was sold a jade pendant and it lead him to believe he's a descendant of the Guhua Clan. However, this pendant is made of Smaragdus Jadeite, which is known to make non-Vision wielders ill. This explained the headaches "Tang Wuchou" claimed to be experiencing.

Appearance[]
Tang Wuchou takes on the form of a Treasure Hoarders: Carmen with a Jadeite Pendant on his hip.

Trivia[]
Before exchanging the egg for the pendant, his name is Tang Wuchou and his title is The Smaragdus Brave.
After completing The Missing Miner World Quest, the player can find Uncle He at Cinnabar Cliff where he would make a final dialogue with "Tang Wuchou" before leaving.
