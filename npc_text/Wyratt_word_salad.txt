Wyratt is an open-world NPC located in the City of Mondstadt, Mondstadt. He can be found guarding one of the doors of the library inside the Knights of Favonius Headquarters.

Appearance[]
Wyratt wears one of the standard uniforms of the Knights of Favonius. The uniform consists of a black long-sleeve shirt and horse riding pants with white plate armor worn on top. However, the poleyns, greaves, and sabatons are black. The Knights of Favonius crest decorates the breastplate.
