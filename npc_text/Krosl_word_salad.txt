Krosl is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the World Quest A Teapot to Call Home: Part I.
He is a Snezhnayan merchant who sought Smaragdus Jadeite to make hairpins, leading Yanfei and the Traveler to seek him out for the rare ore.
