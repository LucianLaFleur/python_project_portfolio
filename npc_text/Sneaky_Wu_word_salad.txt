Sneaky Wu is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Tartaglia's Story Quest Monoceros Caeli Chapter: Act I - Mighty Cyclops' Adventure!.
In Teucer's Terrific Tour in Tartaglia's Story Quest, Monoceros Caeli Chapter: Act I - Mighty Cyclops' Adventure!, he and his two brothers were trying to make a deal with Tartaglia when negotiations broke down as Teucer and the Traveler interrupt the meeting.
