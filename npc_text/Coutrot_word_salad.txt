Profile[]
Kuisel is the namesake for Kuisel's Clockwork Workshop. The Traveler meets Kuisel trapped in a cage, and he asks for help to escape. He initially introduces himself to the Traveler as Coutrot's research partner and claims that Coutrot locked him in the cage. However, it is later revealed that Kuisel and Coutrot are the same person. 
He reveals himself as the real owner of the workshop. He used to work at the Fontaine Research Institute, but was kicked out due to the unethical nature of his research. Bricole was investigating clues about Coutrot's whereabouts.

Appearance[]
He has blonde hair and dons the green and white uniform of the institute.
