Profile[]
In The Adventurers' Guild's Affairs, the Adventurers' Guild receives a commission to find a missing jade-eyed cat, who is Ash. The Traveler is tasked to defeat a Stonehide Lawachurl, eventually finding Ash nearby and then returns it to Lan back in Liyue Harbor.

Appearance[]
Ash has the appearance of a typical Jade-Eyed Cat.
