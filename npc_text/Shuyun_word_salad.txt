Profile[]
When Genius Invokation TCG became popular outside of Inazuma, Shuyun traveled from Liyue[3] to The Cat's Tail and met Prince. He assists Prince and the other cat players in playing their decks when the Traveler challenges them to a duel. He is able to understand what cats say and translates their words to others.
