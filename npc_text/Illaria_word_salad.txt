Illaria is a mentioned character in Genshin Impact. She was mentioned in Ever-Surging Memories in Neuvillette's Story Quest, Diluvies Chapter: Act I - The Remains of the Past Day.

Profile[]
Illaria was the little sister of Vautrin. She passed away when she was young. Vautrin described her to be very similar to Carole in personality: kind, innocent, and believes the best in people, which made her vulnerable to deception and betrayal.[1]
