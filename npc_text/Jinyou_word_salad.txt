Jinyou (Chinese: 晋优 Jìnyōu) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the Archon Quest Chapter II: Prologue - Autumn Winds, Scarlet Leaves.
He is one of the competitors in The Crux Clash, and is the first opponent of the Traveler during the qualifiers.
