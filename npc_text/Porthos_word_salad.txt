Porthos is an open-world NPC located in the City of Mondstadt, Mondstadt. He is a member of the Knights of Favonius and guards the entrance of the Knights of Favonius Headquarters alongside Athos.

Appearance[]
Porthos wears one of the standard uniforms of the Knights of Favonius. The uniform consists of a black long-sleeve shirt and horse riding pants with white plate armor worn on top. However, the poleyns, greaves, and sabatons are black. The Knights of Favonius crest decorates the breastplate.

Trivia[]
He was named after one of the The Three Musketeers. The other characters who follow this naming convention are Athos and Aramis.
