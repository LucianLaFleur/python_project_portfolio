Panicked Myrana is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the Mondstadt Commission Blocked?
She lives in the house southeast of the Dawn Winery Manor, next to the Seelie Court. She is married and has two children, and appears to have a troubled home life, which she deals with by taking walks in the wild.
