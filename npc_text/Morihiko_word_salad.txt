Morihiko (Japanese: 森彦 Morihiko) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Yoimiya's Story Quest Carassius Auratus Chapter: Act I - Dreamlike Timelessness.

Profile[]
Morihiko lives in Inazuma City. He seems to be very close with Yoimiya's family. Yoimiya once helped him solve a dispute between him and his son, and in thanks he sells her fireworks materials and ores.
