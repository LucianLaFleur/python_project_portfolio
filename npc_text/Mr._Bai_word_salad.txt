Profile[]
Mr. Bai, who also went by the name Dr. Baifuzi falsely claimed to be Baizhu's master. Aunt Zhang was familiar with Mr. Bai's true identity. After losing a coin-tossing challenge to Kaeya and the Traveler, Mr. Bai is charged with defrauding Captain Wu, along with many others before him, and is taken away by Jiayi and the Millelith.[1]
