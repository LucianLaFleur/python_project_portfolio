Kina  (Japanese: キナ) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the World Quest series Through the Mists. She also appears as a spirit after the quest series is done on an islet in northwest Autake Plains.

Location[]
During the "Through the Mists" series, Kina can be found on the right side of Moshiri Ceremonial Site, speaking with Kito.

Kina's locationAdditional context
After the completion of the "Through the Mists" series, Kina and Kito can be found as ghostly figures on an islet near Autake Plains. In the Hidden Exploration Objective unofficially named Kito and Kina's Request, they relocate to various places.

Trivia[]
"Kina" (Ainu: キナ kina) is the Ainu word for "grass" or "edible plant." It is also used to refer to a type of grass mat.
