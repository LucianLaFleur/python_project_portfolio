Abe is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the World Quest Series Through the Mists. He also appears as a spirit after the quest series is completed in the ruins between Shirikoro Peak and Chirai Shrine.

Trivia[]
"Abe" likely comes from the common Japanese surname, Abe.
The Japanese name is actually アペ Ape, which may derive from アペ Ape, the Ainu word for "fire."
