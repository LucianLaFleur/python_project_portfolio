Location[]
After completing the Event Story Quest Zip Along (According to Safe Cart Operating Procedures) in Secret Summer Paradise: Part I - An Invitation From Afar!, he can be found operating the Choo-Choo Cart Arena.

Haddo's locationAdditional context
Profile[]
He is described as "the sickly brother" out of his trio of siblings.
Like other characters in the Mirage (besides Idyia), the blue-haired Haddo is a Hydro Eidolon replica of a human from Teyvat that once visited Mirage and then left it.[1]

Appearance[]
Like his brothers, Haddo has watery-blue hair, glowing blue eyes, and wears an Adventurers' Guild uniform. His hair is slightly longer than his brothers' and he has wrinkles under his eyes.
