Uncle Yun (Chinese: 云叔 Yún-shū) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the Teyvat storyline Archon Quests, Chapter II, Prologue: Autumn Winds, Scarlet Leaves.

Profile[]
He is one of the competitors in The Crux Clash. He was eliminated in the third round by Fei the Flyer. Later, he stays as a spectator for the rest of the tournament.
