Profile[]
Quijano is friends with Penny and Nana. He is a swashbuckler wanted by the Maison Gardiennage. He used to adventure with his pals Rouge and Freeman. Three hired hitmen chased them to Poisson, but Quijano saved the day and drove the goons away.[1]

Appearance[]
Don Quijano is a Tiger-Striped Cat with a black hat and a white feather attached to it. He also has a small musketeer-like sword strapped by his side.

Trivia[]
Don Quijano is a reference to the protagonist Alonso Quijano or Don Quixote of the Spanish epic novel El ingenioso hidalgo don Quixote de la Mancha by Miguel de Cervantes.
Don Quijano's appearance may be a reference to Puss in Boots from the Shrek franchise, being an orange cat, having a hat with a feather, and having a Spanish name.
