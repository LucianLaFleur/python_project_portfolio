Shu'yang (Chinese: 舒杨 Shūyáng) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Hu Tao's Story Quest Papilio Charontis Chapter: Act I - Yet the Butterfly Flutters Away. He can be found inside the Story Domain Enter the "border" with Mo.
