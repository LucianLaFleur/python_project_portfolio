Profile[]
Razak is a scholar from Sumeru Akademiya, and is Alhaitham's senior. He was among the scholars kidnapped from Aaru Village to Dar al-Shifa, where the information in their brains were extracted for Knowledge Capsules. He was left behind by his kidnappers in a hurry to flee the hospital, and is later found and returned to the village by the Traveler and Alhaitham.
