Freckle Huang is the leader of the Treasure Hoarders in Liyue. He is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Archon Quest Guizhong in Chapter I: Act II - Farewell, Archaic Lord, and is defeated during the course of the quest. Beyond that, nothing is known of his origins, or how he became the leader of Liyue's Treasure Hoarders.
