Concini is a mentioned character and the boss of Loillier. He is a merchant that hails from Snezhnaya.[1]

Profile[]
Concini had been shipping household goods and materials from Lumidouce Harbor. He and Nacker were working together, and he sent Nacker to Fontaine from Lumidouce Harbor.[1]
Concini and Loillier are financial backers of Edwin Eastinghouse's research.[2]
