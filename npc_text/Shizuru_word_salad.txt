Shizuru  (Japanese: 思鶴 lit. "Thought-Crane") is a quest-exclusive NPC in Inazuma. She can first be found at the Sangonomiya Shrine at Watatsumi Island.
Interacting with her will initiate the Clean House World Quest.

Appearance[]
Shizuru wears the standard shrine maiden uniform consisting of a white kosode and blue hakama, white socks, and okobo sandals with blue straps. Her outfit contains deep blue accents.

Trivia[]
Her name may be a reference to Yoshiya Umitsuru (Japanese: よしや思鶴) (17th or 18th century), also known as Yoshiya Chiruu, a poet in the Ryukyu Kingdom. She is renowned for the Ryuuka-style poetry.
