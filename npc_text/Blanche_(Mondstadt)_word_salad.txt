  Open-World NPCs 




Blanche is a shop NPC in the City of Mondstadt, Mondstadt. She is the shopkeeper of Mondstadt General Goods, indicated by the  icon on the map.
The shop is open at all times of the day and sells cooking ingredients. The stock refreshes every day.

Appearance[]
Blanche is a woman with short brown hair and brown eyes. She wears a brown and beige sleeveless top, olive shoulder less sleeves, olive shorts, a bandage on her right thigh, and black, beige, and olive shoes.
