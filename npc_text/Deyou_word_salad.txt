Profile[]
Deyou is a merchant from Liyue. He is introduced to the Traveler through Freda. The Traveler sells potions to him and helps his business grow.

Appearance[]
Deyou has black hair and eyes, his clothes are reminiscent of Liyue fashion and are in tones of dark blue, light grey and golden accents.
