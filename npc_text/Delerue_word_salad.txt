Profile[]
Delerue is a scientist who works at the Fontaine Research Institute. He is also a reviewer of The Steambird. He tends to write lengthy reviews. Sometimes while writing he will skip on meals, only drinking tea.[1]
Delerue sometimes goes on business trips, asking others to look after Lovi.[2]
