Profile[]
Meng is a relatively new undertaker at the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor. In Papilio Charontis Chapter: Act I - Yet the Butterfly Flutters Away, it is revealed that he initially joined so he could ask the director, Hu Tao, to help him search for his deceased childhood friend Big G. After Big G peacefully moves on, Meng is given unpaid time off to process his feelings.

Appearance[]
Meng is a man with black hair and brown eyes with facial hair on his chin. He wears Wangsheng Funeral Parlor attire.
