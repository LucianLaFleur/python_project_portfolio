Fujita (Japanese: 藤田 Fujita) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the World Quest Shuumatsuban Operations in Series The Very Special Fortune Slip. He may be killed if the Traveler chooses to reveal that they are related to the Shuumatsuban. His real identity is a Fatui Skirmisher - Pyroslinger Bracer.
