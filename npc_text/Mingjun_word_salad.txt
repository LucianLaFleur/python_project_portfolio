Mingjun  (Chinese: 明俊 Míng Jùn) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the Archon Quest Bygones Times Like Dust Passing in Interlude Chapter: Act I - The Crane Returns on the Wind. He was a close friend of Shenhe's father.
