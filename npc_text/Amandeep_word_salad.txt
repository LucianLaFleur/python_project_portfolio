Amandeep is a researcher mentioned by several NPCs in Port Ormos, Sumeru. He graduated from Haravatat, but is also well-versed in Spantamad and Kshahrewar subjects.[1]

Profile[]
Amandeep is a long-time resident of Port Ormos and makes a living by mentoring young researchers.[2]
A few days before the Traveler arrived at Port Ormos, there was a sea monster warning, so Amandeep went out to observe with some Eremites on a ship. Current whereabouts of him, as well as the ship and its crew, are unknown.[2][3][4]

Appearance[]
Amandeep is described as having messy hair and dirty clothes.[2]
