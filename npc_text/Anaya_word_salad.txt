Profile[]
Based on her clothing, Anaya appears to be a Forest Ranger. She is one of three witnesses who recently saw the Sumpter Beastlord: while she was walking in the Dharma Forest, she saw the creature running towards her and fainted. She was later brought to safety with minor wounds.[1]

Appearance[]
Anaya has short brown hair pulled back into two pigtails. She wears the dark green clothes of a Forest Ranger.
