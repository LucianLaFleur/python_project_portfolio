Profile[]
Ladep is a Sumeru Akademiya student from the Spantamad Darshan. Despite belonging to separate Darshans, they collaborate with Amurta student Yasmin and Kshahrewar student Pharez to study the health of Sumeru City's Divine Tree.[1]
