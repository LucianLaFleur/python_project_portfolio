Fleein' Lin (Chinese: 林逃山 Lín Táo Shān) is an event-exclusive NPC and Treasure Hoarder that appears in Dragonspine during the Event World Quest To Thee My Tender Grief Confide in Lunar Realm.
During the event, the player will fight him.
