




Mr. Nine





								Categories
							





  Mentioned Characters 




Mr. Nine is the author of "Legend of the Shattered Halberd" and "Flowers for Princess Fischl." His works are highly popular, and Fischl based her persona on the titular character of "Flowers for Princess Fischl."[1]
