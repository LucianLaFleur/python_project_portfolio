Profile[]
Arapurva is an Aranara. Traveler, Yoimiya, and Avin meet Arapurva after rescuing Avin, who was looking for her old Aranara friend Arashani. Arapurva lets Arashani know about it and the two spirits arrange a meeting between them and Avin in a dream version of Mawtiyima Forest.
