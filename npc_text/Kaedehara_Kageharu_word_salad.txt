Kaedehara Kageharu was Kaedehara Kazuha's father who died prior to the start of the game. He appears in Kazuha's memories during the Event Story Quest As the Courtyard in Spring Once Appeared in Summertime Odyssey:  II - As the Courtyard in Spring Once Appeared.
