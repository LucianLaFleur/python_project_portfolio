Profile[]
Hongru is a waiter at Liuli Pavilion. In An Organization Known as Wangsheng, he and Licai escort Childe, the Traveler, and Paimon inside Liuli Pavilion where Zhongli awaits them.

Appearance[]
Hongru is a man of fair height, wearing a dark blue robe with a blue tinted white vest and cuffs. His short black hair is slicked back, and his eyes are dark brown, framed by circular golden glasses.
