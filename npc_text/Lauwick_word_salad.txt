Profile[]
Traveler meets Lauwick when he is searching for a replacement actress in the troupe's last show to honor their deceased director, Aurelie. Later he reveals that as a child of murderer, he was sent to an orphanage, where he was bullied and beaten. When he ran away, he met Aurelie who took him to her troupe. In "The Little Oceanid" show, he was supposed to play a crane, later recast to a boar due to his appearance.

Appearance[]
Luawick has a large frame, short, dirty blonde hair and yellow eyes. His attire consists of a green suit and white dress shirt, accompanied by grey pants and a pair of brown boots that go mid-way up his calf.
