Profile[]
Hajanad is a determined alchemist who strives to explore the full potential of alchemy, so that she can prove her worth to others and live a better life.

Appearance[]
Hajanad wears the typical attire of a female student in Spantamad. She is of average height and build with brown eyes and fair skin. She has straight brown hair that is cut in a bob with a fringe that covers her forehead.
