Man Chai (Cantonese: 文仔 Man4 Jai2) is Gaming's companion.
He is summoned into battle through Gaming's Elemental Burst, Suanni's Gilded Dance, and also appears in one of Gaming's idle animations.

Man Chai resembles a red suanni in appearance, with red ears, dark paws, and white and green whiskers.

Etymology[]
Man Chai's name is composed of the character 文 man4, "gentle; refined" and the Cantonese diminutive suffix 仔 jai2.
In the English localization, Gaming states that the name means "well-behaved."[1]
