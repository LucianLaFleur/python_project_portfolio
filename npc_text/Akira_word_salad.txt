Akira (Japanese: 晃 Akira) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Arataki Itto's Story Quest Taurus Iracundus Chapter: Act I - Rise Up, Golden Soul.
He first appeared in Character Teaser - "Arataki Itto: That's How the Show Should Go!" alongside fellow Arataki Gang members Genta and Mamoru.
