Hanshirou (Japanese: 半四郎 Hanshirou) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in the Archon Quest Chapter II: Act I - The Immovable God and the Eternal Euthymia.

Profile[]
He received a Vision, but then Okawara Goemon approached him to confiscate it as part of the Vision Hunt Decree. However, it turned out that Hanshirou did not give away his real vision, but a fake one that he received from Yoimiya who in turn got it from Master Masakatsu.
