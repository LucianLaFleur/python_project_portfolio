  Quest-Exclusive NPCs 




Little Le is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in For A Child's Smile in Chongyun's Hangout Event: Act I - Signs of Evil.
She is Dalong's younger sister and Yuyan's daughter.
When playing hide and seek, she is located behind the tree by the glaze lilies at the nearby Teleport Waypoint.
