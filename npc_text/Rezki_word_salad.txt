Profile[]
Rezki was one of Babel's assassins. The Matriarch sent him and Nayram to kill the Traveler in order to secure possession of Liloupar as part of her plan to occupy The Orchard of Pairidaeza. Nayram and Rezki failed in their mission, being defeated by Jeht and the Traveler.[1]

Appearance[]
Rezki takes on the appearance of an Eremite Sunfrost.
