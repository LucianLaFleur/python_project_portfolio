Imatani Kaori (Japanese: 今谷香里 Imatani Kaori) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Yoimiya's Story Quest Carassius Auratus Chapter: Act I - Dreamlike Timelessness.

Profile[]
Imatani Kaori is married to her husband, Imatani Saburou. They live in Konda Village together. They have a son named Imatani Keisuke who works with the Tenryou Commission.
