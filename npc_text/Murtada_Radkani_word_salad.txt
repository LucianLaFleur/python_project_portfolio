Profile[]
Murtada is the son of Taj Radkani. At some point prior to the events of the game, wanting to surpass his father, Murtada was enticed by an unknown organization to research the Court of Desolation, which was prohibited in the Akademiya. Murtada was arrested by his own father and thrown into jail, where he subsequently committed suicide.
