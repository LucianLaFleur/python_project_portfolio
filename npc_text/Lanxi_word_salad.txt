Lanxi (Chinese: 兰溪 Lánxī) is a quest-exclusive NPC that appears in Hu Tao's Story Quest Papilio Charontis Chapter: Act I - Yet the Butterfly Flutters Away. She can be found inside the Story Domain Enter the "border," waiting for a certain "someone" to finally cross over.
