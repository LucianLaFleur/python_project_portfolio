Crimson Cyclone is an unusually large and discolored Onikabuto that was captured by Paimon. Due to its size, it is able to easily flip other Onikabuto on their backs. It is currently owned by Daisuke as his pet. It appears in The Oni's Justice in Arataki Itto's Story Quest, Taurus Iracundus Chapter: Act I - Rise Up, Golden Soul.
