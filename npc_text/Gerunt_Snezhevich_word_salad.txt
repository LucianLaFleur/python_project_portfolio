Profile[]
Gerunt Snezhevich is a servant of Arlecchino and helped save lives in the Spina di Rosula's base at Poisson. Navia is grateful for Arlecchino and Gerunt's help. Gerunt is also a big fan of Charlotte's writing in The Steambird.[2]

Appearance[]
He has gray hair and dons the black and red uniform of a Fatui Pyro Agent.
